Maninder Sehgal

Economist  |  Data Scientist

I work at the intersection of economic policy and data analysis, with a focus on Canada's macroeconomic landscape — labour markets, inflation dynamics, fiscal policy, and provincial economic performance.

My work combines rigorous quantitative methods with clear, decision-ready communication, drawing on government and statistical agency data to surface actionable insights for policy audiences.

I have a particular interest in how macroeconomic trends affect everyday Canadians and in building transparent, reproducible data tools that make complex indicators accessible.
